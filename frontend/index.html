Who We Are

About Ekalavya Performing Arts

Ekalavya Performing Arts is a Bengaluru-based theatre collective of passionate artists, directors, musicians, and storytellers.

We blend tradition with modern creativity — exploring society, human emotions, and culture through drama, mime, music, and dance. Based in Bengaluru with roots in Kolkata.
