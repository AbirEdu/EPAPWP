Who We Are

Ekalavya Performing Arts

Where Passion Meets Performance

Theatre Productions

Original and adapted productions exploring human emotions, relationships, and contemporary social themes through compelling storytelling and innovative stagecraft.

Mime & Non-Verbal Theatre

Through mime, physical theatre, and expressive movement, we create performances that transcend language and communicate universally.

Ekalavya Performing Arts (EPA) is a vibrant theatre collective dedicated to nurturing creativity through drama, mime, music, dance, and interdisciplinary performing arts.

Founded by a passionate group of art enthusiasts, EPA has grown into a dynamic cultural organization that inspires artists of all ages while creating meaningful performances that connect with audiences. With a strong presence in Bengaluru, and performances across Kolkata, Pune, and Delhi, EPA has established itself as a platform where artistic expression, learning, and community come together.

Workshops & Training

Regular workshops in acting, mime, voice, movement, improvisation, and stage presence — for beginners and experienced performers alike.

Cultural Events

Active participation in theatre festivals, community performances, educational programs, and cultural collaborations that celebrate artistic diversity.

Our Vision

To cultivate a thriving performing arts community that encourages creativity, collaboration, and cultural appreciation while inspiring individuals to discover confidence, empathy, and self-expression through theatre.

Our Mission
Promote theatre and performing arts across all age groups.
Provide structured training in drama, mime, movement, voice, and stagecraft.
Encourage young performers to develop creativity, confidence, and communication skills.
Produce meaningful performances that reflect social, cultural, and human values.
Build an inclusive artistic community where experienced artists and emerging talent learn together.

Our Productions Wing

Picture Wicture Productions

The cinematic and documentary arm of Ekalavya — capturing performances, short films, and cultural stories.

A creative venture established in 2019, where artistry meets entertainment with boundless creativity. PWP has carved a distinctive niche in short films and contemporary music through original storytelling and compelling social commentary — exploring the intricacies of human relationships and tackling powerful themes of integrity, infidelity, and nationalism. Our work has garnered multiple awards and Official Selections at prestigious film festivals.

Short Films

Socially conscious short films exploring Bengali culture, urban stories, and human emotions.

Documentaries

Behind-the-scenes and stage documentaries preserving our theatrical heritage on film.

Performance Recordings

High-quality recordings of live performances for archival and online audiences worldwide.
