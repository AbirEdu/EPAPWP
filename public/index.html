Our Founders

Biplab Kundu

Co-Founder & Director. Biplab is a passionate actor, director, who balances his IT career with theatre. A founder of Ekalavya Performing Arts, he has performed and directed numerous acclaimed plays, mimes, and fusions across Bengaluru and Kolkata stages.

Bodhisatta Sarkar

Co-Founder & Screen play, Director. Bodhisatta, a senior IT professional and passionate theatre artist, is a founder of Ekalavya Performing Arts. Known for his powerful scripts and direction, he creates socially conscious plays and mimes exploring human emotions, relationships, and contemporary issues across Bengaluru stages.

Subarna Kundu

Co-founder & Actor. Subarna, a successful entrepreneur, homemaker, and mother, is also a passionate theatre artist. A founder of Ekalavya Performing Arts, she has performed acclaimed plays, mimes, and pantomimes across Bengaluru and Kolkata, inspiring cultural excellence.
